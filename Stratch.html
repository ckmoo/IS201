About Me

Chalise was born in Salt Lake City and loves skiing and boating in the beautiful mountains of Utah. She loves traveling and has been all over the world, but her favorite countries are Italy, Peru, France, and most of all, Brazil! Chalise served a mission in the south of Brazil and has been back to visit a few times. She is so excited to go back again with HXP this summer! Chalise loves technology and innovation, and launched a tech start-up focused on easing the process of finding Assisted Living for a loved one. The company has won numerous awards as part of BYU's 2023 Sandbox co-hort. Chalise will finish her major in Finance from the BYU Marriott School of Business this April. Following graduation, Chalise plans to join Peterson Partners, a Private Equity firm based in Salt Lake City. If you know Chalise, she has probably talked you ear off about her "top restaurants of all-time list," convinced you to come skiing, pulled you into an icecream shop, or is out training for her next marathon.
